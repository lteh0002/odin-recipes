Nasi Lemak

Aromatic coconut steamed rice served with chicken curry, sambal, anchovies, boiled egg and fresh cucumbers!

A dish originating in Malay cuisine commonly found in Malaysia, where it is considered the national dish.

The ingredients are

  • Sambal
  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chili Pepper
  • Pandan
  • Onion
  • Anchovies
  • Salt
  • Peanuts
  • Garlic
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Ginger
  • Cucumber
  • Shallot

Steps

  1. Prepare the Coconut Milk Rice.
  2. Add Water and Watery Coconut Milk Into Rice.
  3. Cook the Rice
  4. Prepare to Cook the Chilli Anchovies using
    • Shallots
    • Chili Paste
    • Garlic
    • Water
    • Anchovies
    • Salt
    • Onions
  5. Blend the Chillies, Shallots and Garlic.
  6. Deep Fry the Anchovies.
  7. Cook Hard Boiled Eggs and Slice Cucumber.
  8. Assemble the Coconut Milk Rice (Nasi Lemak)
