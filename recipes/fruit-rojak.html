Fruit Rojak

A Malaysian Spicy Fruit Salad with a mix of flavors and textures unlike any other.

It is spicy, sweet, salty, tangy, soft, and crunchy. A must try!

The ingredients are

  • Pineapple
  • Apple
  • Green Mango
  • Guava
  • Ambarella
  • Jicama
  • Cucumber
  • Shrimp paste

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce
  2. Cut the fruits in pieces and assemble the fruits
  3. Drizzle the rojak sauce onto the fruits
